Pasta Zucchine e Pancetta

Ingredients

  • 400gr Pasta
  • 3 Zucchini
  • 150gr Pancetta
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Parmezan Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cook 2/3 of the zuchinni with one onion until slightly brown
  2. Add some basil leaves and parmezan cheese
  3. Throw everything together in a blender and add a bit of Olive Oil
  4. Blend into a sauce and set aside for later
  5. Put the pancetta in the pan, once they start to become slightly brown add the other onion and garlic
  6. Cook the pasta until al dente
  7. Once the pancetta is nice and crispy and the onion and garlic have cooked throw in the pasta
  8. Add the sauce and enjoy
